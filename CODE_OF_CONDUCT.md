# Code of Conduct

The Tech Visionaries Network (TVN) is dedicated to providing a harassment-free, collaborative, and professional experience for everyone.

---

## Our Standards

**Expected Behavior:**
- Demonstrate empathy and kindness toward other builders.
- Give and gracefully accept constructive technical feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the engineering community.
- Use professional, inclusive language in pull requests, issues, and code reviews.

**Unacceptable Behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or insulting/derogatory comments.
- Public or private harassment of any community member.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Dismissive or elitist behavior regarding technical experience levels.

## Code Review Etiquette

Code reviews are for improving code, not tearing down the author.
- Critique the code, not the developer.
- Explain the reasoning behind your requested changes.
- Offer solutions or pointers to documentation when pointing out an error.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the repository administrators. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, or to ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
